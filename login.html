🔒 Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: March 2026 · STRATIX v3.0

1. What is STRATIX?

STRATIX is a complete business intelligence platform for Indian entrepreneurs. All core features work offline. Your data belongs to you — we never sell it.

2. Data We Collect

Account: Name, email, business name, phone (for login only).

Business Data: Revenue, expenses, employees, invoices — stored only on your device (localStorage) by default. Never sent to any server unless you connect Firebase.

Passwords: Hashed with a one-way algorithm — never stored in plain text.

3. We Do NOT Sell Your Data

STRATIX never sells, trades, or shares your business data with any third party. We may share limited info only with:
• Payment processors (when payments are enabled)
• Legal authorities (only if required by Indian law)

4. AI Advisor (Claude AI)

When you use AI Advisor, your business summary (revenue/expense totals) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API using your own API key. We do not store these conversations. Anthropic's privacy policy applies.

5. WhatsApp Integration

Messages are composed in STRATIX and sent via WhatsApp Web (wa.me links). We do not access your WhatsApp account. Content stays between you and your clients.

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

✅ Access all your data  •  ✅ Correct inaccurate info  •  ✅ Delete your account (Settings → Clear Data)  •  ✅ Export all data as JSON (Settings → Export)  •  ✅ Raise grievance: Stratixapp@gmail.com

7. Terms of Service

8. Financial Disclaimer

GST calculations, PF/ESI/TDS, loan estimates and profit projections are indicative only. Always verify with a qualified Chartered Accountant before filing returns or making financial decisions.
